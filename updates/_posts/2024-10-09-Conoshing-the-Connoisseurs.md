Alex Provo of NYU Libraries and the Semantic Lab at Pratt presented at the 2024 LD4 Conference on October 9th. Her talk, *[Conoshing the Connoisseurs: The Early Writings of Bernard and Mary Berenson,](https://2024ld4.sched.com/event/1lv1c/conoshing-the-connoisseurs-a-joint-bibliography-of-bernard-and-mary-berenson)* gave an overview of the currnet bibliography and text analysis project which aims to reveal a particular “culture” of connoisseurship in the late nineteenth century.
