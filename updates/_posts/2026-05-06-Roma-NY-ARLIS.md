title: "Roma / New York Pilot Project Lightning Talk at ARLIS/NA Conference"
---
Semantic Lab Research Fellow and Pratt SI alum Calista Donohoe reported on the Roma / New York: 1948–1964 pilot project in a lightning talk at the Art Libraries of North America conference in Montréal. The title of her lightning talk was “[Mapping Post-War Artistic Networks with Semantic Technologies]((https://drive.google.com/file/d/1VRhwrAN6GVH2tq8B_vtarmVURgS3BX1z/view?usp=drive_link))".
